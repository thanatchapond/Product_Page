Welcome

to the world of Exotic Pets!













”

Exotic pets are animals that are not considered
common household pets.
They are often rare, originate from foreign countries,
or have unique characteristics
that require special care.


”

Types of Exotic Pets

1

Reptiles –

such as snakes, geckos, chameleons, and turtles.

2

Amphibians –

including frogs, salamanders, and newts.

3

Birds –

like parrots, macaws, and cockatoos.

4

Small Mammals –

such as sugar gliders, hedgehogs, and ferrets.

5

Invertebrates –

including tarantulas, scorpions, and exotic beetles.
