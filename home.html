Wallaby

Wallabies are small kangaroo like marsupials from Australia. They’re gentle, shy, and need spacious care.

Ferret

Ferrets are playful, curious, and intelligent mammals. They love to explore and interact with people.

Ball Pythons

Ball Pythons are calm, non-venomous snakes that are easy to handle. Great for beginner reptile keepers.
